Workout Plans

Workout Plans
Week 1: Foundation

Day 1: Full-Body Strength Training

#

Warm-up (5-10 min): Jumping jacks, bodyweight squats, arm circles.

Workout : Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
Push-ups (modified if needed): 3 sets of 10 reps
Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps (each side)
Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 15 reps
Plank (hold for 30-60 seconds)

Day 2: Cardio & Core

Warm-up (5-10 min): Brisk walk or light jog.

Workout : 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio (cycling, brisk walking, swimming, or jogging)
Core circuit (repeat 2-3 times):
Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds
Russian Twists: 20 reps (each side)
Leg Raises: 15 reps
Bicycle Crunches: 20 reps


Cooldown: Walking and core stretches.

Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery

Day 4: Upper Body Strength & Flexibility

Warm-up (5-10 min): Arm swings, shoulder rolls, dynamic stretches.

Workout : Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 15 reps
Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (or wall push-ups)

Cooldown: Arm, shoulder, and chest stretches.

Day 5: Cardio & Lower Body

#

Warm-up (5-10 min): Light jogging or jump rope.

Workout : 30-40 minutes of cycling, running, or swimming
Lower body strength circuit (repeat 2-3 times):
Lunges: 12 reps each side
Step-ups (on a bench or stair): 12 reps each leg
Calf Raises: 15 reps
Side Lunges: 12 reps each side

Cooldown: Stretch quads, hamstrings, and calves.
